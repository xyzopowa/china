Where to stay in Beijing

5

6

Beijing is a sprawling city, so where you stay can really shape your experience. If you’re keen to be close to the big-ticket sights like the

[Forbidden City](https://emrldtp.com/re?campaign_id=47&marker=280299&p=1922&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.viator.com%2Fx-attractions%2Fy%2Fd0-a169%3Fapi_version%3D2.0&trs=185129&trace_id=Zz6ebcb54aef5a4b988d5b972-280299&install_type=am_partner_content&product_type=emerald&journey_id=nUEqK3XW_lqY4tahpMRSZ&promo_kind=tp_other&page_url=https%3A%2F%2Flittlegreybox.net%2F21-awesome-things-to-do-and-avoid-in-beijing%2F)

and

[Tiananmen Square](https://emrldtp.com/re?campaign_id=84&marker=280299&p=2076&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.booking.com%2Fsearchresults.en-gb.html%3Fdest_id%3D13016%26dest_type%3Dlandmark&trs=185129&trace_id=Zz6ebcb54aef5a4b988d5b972-280299&install_type=am_partner_content&product_type=emerald&journey_id=cSwA0b1xxYxxImxzlDqPQ&promo_kind=tp_other&page_url=https%3A%2F%2Flittlegreybox.net%2F21-awesome-things-to-do-and-avoid-in-beijing%2F)

, Dongcheng District is ideal. For a mix of traditional charm and cool, creative energy, the hutongs around Gulou and

[Nanluoguxiang](https://emrldtp.com/re?campaign_id=47&marker=280299&p=1922&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.viator.com%2Fx-attractions%2Fy%2Fd0-a10140%3Fapi_version%3D2.0&trs=185129&trace_id=Zz6ebcb54aef5a4b988d5b972-280299&install_type=am_partner_content&product_type=emerald&journey_id=-pgVNLaTSGiCFo9bcLMtK&promo_kind=tp_other&page_url=https%3A%2F%2Flittlegreybox.net%2F21-awesome-things-to-do-and-avoid-in-beijing%2F)

are great. If modern luxury, shopping and nightlife are more your style, head to

[Chaoyang](https://emrldtp.com/re?campaign_id=84&marker=280299&p=2076&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.booking.com%2Fsearchresults.en-gb.html%3Fdest_id%3D1718%26dest_type%3Ddistrict&trs=185129&trace_id=Zz6ebcb54aef5a4b988d5b972-280299&install_type=am_partner_content&product_type=emerald&journey_id=cxZjclY895eJMGTGypFSj&promo_kind=tp_other&page_url=https%3A%2F%2Flittlegreybox.net%2F21-awesome-things-to-do-and-avoid-in-beijing%2F)

or

[Sanlitun](https://emrldtp.com/re?campaign_id=84&marker=280299&p=2076&u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.booking.com%2Fsearchresults.en-gb.html%3Fdest_id%3D2858%26dest_type%3Ddistrict&trs=185129&trace_id=Zz6ebcb54aef5a4b988d5b972-280299&install_type=am_partner_content&product_type=emerald&journey_id=qkgAehXC90StNK3DzLSnO&promo_kind=tp_other&page_url=https%3A%2F%2Flittlegreybox.net%2F21-awesome-things-to-do-and-avoid-in-beijing%2F)

. Wherever you base yourself, make sure it’s close to a metro station—Beijing’s subway is a lifesaver when it comes to getting around.
