One of China's finest museums with an extensive collection of Chinese art and artifacts.

### Collections

- **Ancient Chinese Art**: Bronzes, ceramics, jade
- **Calligraphy & Painting**: Classical Chinese artworks
- **Traditional Furniture**: Ming and Qing Dynasty pieces
- **Coins**: Comprehensive Chinese currency history
- **Ethnic Minorities**: Cultural artifacts from China's diverse peoples

### Visiting Information

- **Location**: People's Square, central Shanghai
- **Entry**: Free (advance booking required)
- **Duration**: 2-4 hours recommended
- **Language**: English descriptions available

### Practical Tips

- **Book Ahead**: Reserve online several days in advance
- **Best Time**: Weekday mornings for fewer crowds
- **Audio Guide**: Available in multiple languages
- **Photography**: Allowed in most areas (no flash)

- People's Square
- Urban Planning Exhibition Center
