## Nanluoguxiang — Hutongs & Night Markets

Nanluoguxiang is one of Beijing's most photographed hutong streets — a compact, atmospheric alleyway full of shops, snacks, and photo opportunities.

### Hutongs

Nanluoguxiang Hutong is a beautiful area to visit and a must-see for travellers. Explore winding streets, colourful architecture, snack stalls, cafes, and boutiques.

[Nanluoguxiang Hutong](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g294212-d668414-Reviews-Nanluoguxiang-Beijing.html)

### Night Markets

The area comes alive in the evening — street food stalls, souvenirs, and a lively local atmosphere make Nanluoguxiang a great spot for a night-time stroll and sampling local bites.

Tips:
- Arrive earlier in the evening to beat peak crowds.
- Try local snacks and small-plate stalls along the lane.
