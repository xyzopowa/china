## Xi'an City Center

The historic center of Xi'an, enclosed by the ancient city walls, contains numerous attractions that showcase the city's rich imperial history and diverse cultural heritage.

## Ancient City Wall (城墙)

### Overview
- **Built**: Ming Dynasty (1370-1378)
- **Length**: 13.7km complete circuit
- **Height**: 12m high, 15-18m wide
- **Best Preserved**: Most complete city fortification system in China

### Activities
- **Cycling**: Rent bikes, complete circuit in 2-3 hours (¥45/2 hours)
- **Walking**: Free once on wall, full circuit takes 4-5 hours
- **Night Visit**: Beautiful lighting (included in day ticket)
- **Gates**: Four main gates, South Gate most impressive

### Visitor Info
- **Admission**: ¥54
- **Hours**: 8 AM - 10 PM (summer), 8 AM - 7 PM (winter)
- **Best Time**: Late afternoon into evening
- **Access Points**: South Gate (main), other cardinal gates

## Muslim Quarter (回民街)

### Overview
The heart of Xi'an's Muslim community, this bustling area is famous for street food, shopping, and cultural atmosphere.

### Main Streets
- **Beiyuanmen**: Main tourist street
- **Dapi Yuan**: More authentic, less touristy
- **Xiyang Shi**: Local favorite for food

### Must-Try Foods
- **Yangrou Paomo** (羊肉泡馍): Lamb soup with bread
- **Roujiamo** (肉夹馍): Chinese hamburger
- **Biangbiang Noodles**: Wide hand-pulled noodles
- **Liangpi** (凉皮): Cold skin noodles
- **Persimmon Cakes**: Local sweet specialty
- **Pomegranate Juice**: Freshly squeezed

### Attractions
- **Great Mosque**: One of China's largest and oldest mosques
  - Admission: ¥25
  - Hours: 8 AM - 7 PM
  - Architecture: Chinese-Islamic fusion style

### Shopping
- **Souvenirs**: Calligraphy, jade, shadow puppets
- **Handicrafts**: Traditional Xi'an specialties
- **Bargaining**: Expected and encouraged

## Bell Tower (钟楼)

### Overview
- **Built**: 1384 (Ming Dynasty)
- **Height**: 36m
- **Location**: Exact center of ancient Xi'an
- **Admission**: ¥30 (¥50 combo with Drum Tower)

### Features
- Massive bronze bell
- Musical performances (several times daily)
- 360-degree city views
- Historic exhibits

## Drum Tower (鼓楼)

### Overview
- **Built**: 1380 (Ming Dynasty)  
- **Height**: 34m
- **Location**: West of Bell Tower
- **Admission**: ¥30 (¥50 combo with Bell Tower)

### Features
- Ancient drums collection
- Drum performances
- Views of Muslim Quarter
- Traditional architecture

## Temple Sites

### Big Wild Goose Pagoda (大雁塔)
- **Built**: 652 AD (Tang Dynasty)
- **Purpose**: House Buddhist scriptures from India
- **Admission**: ¥50 (pagoda ¥40, temple ¥50)
- **Features**: 
  - 64m tall, seven stories
  - Climbing to top permitted
  - Musical fountain shows (free, outside temple)
  - Large Buddhist temple complex

### Small Wild Goose Pagoda (小雁塔)
- **Built**: 707 AD
- **Admission**: Free (park ¥50 to climb pagoda)
- **Features**:
  - Quieter than Big Pagoda
  - Beautiful gardens
  - Museum on-site

## Other Attractions

### Shaanxi History Museum
- **Admission**: Free (online reservation required)
- **Hours**: 8:30 AM - 6 PM (closed Mondays)
- **Duration**: 2-3 hours
- **Highlights**: Tang Dynasty treasures, ancient artifacts

### Tang Paradise
- **Type**: Tang Dynasty theme park
- **Admission**: ¥120
- **Best For**: Evening light shows
- **Duration**: 2-3 hours

### Forest of Stone Steles Museum
- **Admission**: ¥65
- **Features**: Ancient calligraphy, Buddhist sculptures
- **Best For**: Calligraphy enthusiasts, historians

## Getting Around

### Metro
- **Lines**: Multiple lines serve historic center
- **Stations**: Bell Tower (interchange), Yongning Men (South Gate)
- **Cost**: ¥2-7 depending on distance

### Bike Sharing
- Apps available (Mobike, Hellobike)
- Convenient for exploring within walls

### Walking
- Historic center very walkable
- Most sights within 3km of each other

## Recommended Itineraries

### Half Day
1. Morning: City Wall cycling (2-3 hours)
2. Lunch: Muslim Quarter
3. Afternoon: Bell & Drum Towers

### Full Day
1. Morning: Shaanxi History Museum (2-3 hours)
2. Lunch: Local restaurant
3. Afternoon: Big Wild Goose Pagoda
4. Late Afternoon: City Wall cycling
5. Evening: Muslim Quarter dinner and shopping

### Two Days
**Day 1**: Terracotta Warriors + evening in Muslim Quarter  
**Day 2**: City Wall + Historic center attractions

## Dining Beyond Muslim Quarter

- **De Fa Chang**: Famous for jiaozi (dumplings)
- **Huimin Street**: Near Muslim Quarter, more local
- **Food Streets**: Various around city for local specialties

## Tips

- **Muslim Quarter**: Visit evening for best atmosphere
- **City Wall**: Bike easier than walking full circuit
- **Combo Tickets**: Save money on multiple attractions
- **Peak Times**: Avoid Chinese holidays and weekends
- **Bargaining**: Expected in markets and some shops
