## Beijing Cuisine

Beijing's culinary scene offers a rich tapestry of flavors, from imperial cuisine to street food delights.

### Signature Dishes

#### Peking Duck (Beijing Kaoya)
The most famous Beijing dish - crispy skinned duck served with thin pancakes, scallions, and sweet bean sauce.

![Peking Duck](/images/beijing/peking_duck.jpg)

Peking duck features ultra-crispy skin and juicy meat; traditionally carved tableside and wrapped in thin pancakes with scallions and hoisin or sweet bean sauce.

Image: [Peking duck](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Peking_duck.jpg) — Mokkie / CC BY-SA 3.0.

#### Jiaozi (Dumplings)
Traditional northern-style dumplings filled with pork and vegetables.

![Jiaozi](/images/beijing/jiaozi.jpg)

Jiaozi are boiled or pan-fried dumplings commonly served at breakfast or as a staple meal; fillings vary seasonally.

Image: [Jiaozi](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Jiaozi.jpg) — Gene.arboit / CC BY-SA / GFDL (see file page).

#### Zhajiangmian
Hand-pulled noodles with savory soybean paste sauce, a local favorite.

![Zhajiangmian](/images/beijing/zhajiangmian.jpg)

Zhajiangmian are wheat noodles tossed in a chunky fermented soybean and meat sauce, topped with fresh cucumber and radish for contrast.

Image: [Zhajiangmian 20180501](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Zhajiangmian_20180501.jpg) — Hal_0005 / CC BY-SA 4.0.

### Street Food

- **Jianbing**: Savory crepes with egg, crispy wonton, scallions, and sauces
- **Tanghulu**: Candied fruit skewers, especially hawthorn berries
- **Baozi**: Steamed buns with various fillings

![Jianbing](/images/beijing/jianbing.jpg)

Jianbing is a breakfast crepe folded around egg, crunchy wafer and sauces — a perfect on-the-go snack.

Image: [Jianbing being cooked](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Jianbing_1.jpg) — Ernie / CC BY 2.0.

### Where to Eat

#### Traditional Restaurants
Look for established Peking duck restaurants in Dongcheng District.

#### Night Markets
Experience authentic street food at Wangfujing Snack Street or Donghuamen Night Market.

#### Hutong Eateries
Small family-run restaurants in hutongs offer authentic home-style cooking.

### Dining Tips

- Reservations recommended for famous restaurants
- Learn basic food phrases in Mandarin
- Be adventurous but know your spice tolerance
- Tea houses offer traditional Beijing snacks
