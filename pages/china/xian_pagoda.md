## Small Wild Goose Pagoda
### Petite Pagode de l'Oie Sauvage

A beautiful Tang Dynasty pagoda offering a quieter alternative to its larger counterpart.

### What to See

- Historic Buddhist pagoda structure
- Peaceful temple gardens
- Ancient architecture
- Morning bell rituals

### Visiting Information

- **Location**: Xi'an city
- **Time needed**: 1-2 hours
- **Best time**: Morning for peaceful atmosphere
