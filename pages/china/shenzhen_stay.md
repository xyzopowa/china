## Best Neighborhoods to Stay

### Futian District (福田区) ★★★★★

**Best For**: Central location, business, shopping

**Character**: Modern business district, central, well-connected

**Pros**:
- Excellent metro connections (multiple lines)
- Near Civic Center and CBD
- Shopping (Coco Park, other malls)
- Good restaurant variety
- Central to most attractions
- Close to Hong Kong border (Futian crossing)

**Cons**:
- Can feel corporate
- Busy and crowded
- Less character

**Recommended For**: First-time visitors, business travelers

**Distance to Key Areas**:
- Hong Kong border (Futian): 10 minutes metro
- Theme parks: 30-45 minutes
- Shekou: 40 minutes metro

### Luohu District (罗湖区) ★★★★

**Best For**: Hong Kong visitors, shopping, convenience

**Character**: Older district, shopping focus, border area

**Pros**:
- Right at Hong Kong border (Luohu crossing)
- Major shopping (Dongmen, Lo Wu Commercial City)
- Easy access from Hong Kong
- Good hotel value
- Plenty of dining options
- Metro access

**Cons**:
- Older, less modern
- Can feel touristy
- Not the "real" Shenzhen

**Recommended For**: Hong Kong day-trippers, shoppers, budget travelers

**Distance to Key Areas**:
- Hong Kong border (Luohu): Walking distance
- Futian: 10 minutes metro
- Theme parks: 40-50 minutes

### Nanshan District / Shekou (南山区/蛇口) ★★★★★

**Best For**: Expats, international vibe, quality of life

**Character**: Expat enclave, international, relaxed

**Pros**:
- International community
- Western restaurants and bars
- Expat-friendly amenities
- Seaworld area (not an aquarium, a plaza)
- OCT area (theme parks nearby)
- More "livable" feel
- English more common

**Cons**:
- Further from other districts
- More expensive
- Less "Chinese" experience

**Recommended For**: Those seeking Western comfort, expats, families

**Distance to Key Areas**:
- Hong Kong border: 40-50 minutes
- Central Futian: 30-40 minutes
- OCT/Window of the World: 10-20 minutes

### Bao'an District (宝安区) ★★

**Best For**: Airport proximity only

**Character**: Industrial, airport area

**Pros**:
- Near airport
- Budget options

**Cons**:
- Far from attractions
- Industrial feel
- Inconvenient for tourism

**Recommended For**: Early flights only, not for general tourism

### Coco Park Area (Futian) ★★★★

**Best For**: Nightlife, dining, shopping, young travelers

**Character**: Trendy dining and nightlife hub

**Pros**:
- Best restaurant selection
- Nightlife
- Modern and vibrant
- Good shopping
- Metro connected
- Young, energetic vibe

**Cons**:
- Can be loud at night
- More expensive dining

**Recommended For**: Foodies, nightlife seekers, young travelers

## Booking Considerations

### When to Book

**Normal Times**:
- 1-2 weeks ahead
- Good availability
- Better rates

**Chinese Holidays**:
- 2-4 weeks ahead
- Higher prices
- Book early

**Trade Shows/Events**:
- Check event calendar
- Book as early as possible

### What to Look For

1. **Metro access**: Essential for convenience
2. **Neighborhood**: Match to your needs (business, expat, shopping)
3. **English service**: If needed
4. **Recent reviews**: Quality check
5. **Breakfast options**: Western available?
6. **Facilities**: Pool, gym if important

### Red Flags

- Too cheap (likely quality issues)
- Far from metro
- Lots of cleanliness complaints
- No recent renovations
- In industrial areas (unless near airport)

## Amenities Guide

### Breakfast

**International Hotels**:
- Western + Asian buffet
- Coffee, juice
- High quality
- Worth the hotel choice

**Chinese Hotels**:
- Chinese breakfast (congee, buns)
- Limited Western
- Basic
- Often included

**Budget**:
- Usually not included
- Better to eat out

### Wi-Fi
- Standard in all hotels
- Quality varies
- International hotels most reliable
- VPN may be needed

### Air Conditioning
- Essential in summer
- All hotels have it
- Can be very cold (bring layers)

## Check-In Requirements

- **Passport**: Required for foreigners
- **Registration**: Automatic police registration
- **Deposit**: Sometimes required
- **Check-in**: Usually 2:00 PM
- **Check-out**: Usually 12:00 noon

## Cost-Saving Tips

1. **Book in advance**: Better rates
2. **Stay in Luohu/Futian**: Better value than Shekou
3. **Weekdays**: Cheaper than weekends (sometimes)
4. **Book directly**: Sometimes better than OTAs
5. **Compare platforms**: Ctrip, Booking.com, hotel direct
6. **Longer stays**: Negotiate for 3+ nights
