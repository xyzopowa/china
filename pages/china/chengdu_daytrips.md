## Day Trips from Chengdu

### Mount Emei (1-2 hours)
### Ã€ 1-2h

One of China's Four Sacred Buddhist Mountains.

**Highlights**:
- Ancient Buddhist temples
- Wild monkeys
- Cable car to summit
- Sunrise views (if staying overnight)
- Hiking trails

**Duration**: Full day or overnight  
**Access**: Bus or tour from Chengdu

### Leshan Giant Buddha (1-2 hours)

Already covered in dedicated page - can combine with Mount Emei for 2-day trip.

### Mount Qingcheng (Train accessible)

**Birthplace of Taoism**
- Taoist temples and shrines
- Mountain hiking
- Cable car available
- Peaceful atmosphere
- Less crowded than Mount Emei

**Duration**: Half to full day  
**Access**: High-speed train from Chengdu

### Jiuzhaigou (8h bus)

**Turquoise Lakes and Waterfalls**
- UNESCO World Heritage site
- Spectacular natural beauty
- Colorful pools
- Best April-November
- Requires overnight stay

**Duration**: 3-4 days recommended  
**Season**: Closed in winter

### Huanglong

**Colorful Travertine Pools**
- Multicolored calcite pools
- High altitude (bring warm clothes)
- Often combined with Jiuzhaigou
- Stunning mountain scenery

**Duration**: Can combine with Jiuzhaigou  
**Access**: Long bus journey or fly

### Planning Tips

- **Short on time**: Mount Qingcheng or Leshan
- **Nature lovers**: Jiuzhaigou/Huanglong (3-4 days)
- **Cultural**: Mount Emei (1-2 days)
- **Easy access**: Mount Qingcheng (half day)

### Transportation

- Tours simplest for distant locations
- High-speed trains for nearby mountains
- Book multi-day trips through agencies
