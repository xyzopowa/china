## Hangzhou Day Trip
### Sites de Hangzhou

1.5-2 hours from Shanghai by high-speed train.

### Getting There

**High-Speed Train**
- 1.5-2 hour journey
- Frequent departures
- Book 1-2 weeks ahead for weekends
- Hangzhou East Station most convenient

### West Lake (UNESCO)
### Lac de l'Ouest

Iconic scenic lake that has inspired Chinese poets for centuries.

**Highlights**:
- Poetic landscapes
- Pagodas and bridges
- Boat rides
- Lakeside walks
- Seasonal beauty (lotus flowers, autumn leaves)

**Activities**:
- Rent bicycle to circle the lake
- Take boat to islands
- Visit Su Causeway and Bai Causeway
- Leifeng Pagoda for panoramic views

### Lingyin Temple

Ancient Buddhist temple complex nestled in wooded hills.

**Features**:
- Founded in 326 AD
- Beautiful architecture
- Feilai Feng grottoes with carved Buddhas
- Peaceful forest setting
- Multiple halls and courtyards

### Longjing Tea Plantations
### Longjing Tea Plantations

Famous Dragon Well tea region.

**Experience**:
- Walk through tea terraces
- Watch tea picking (spring season)
- Tea tasting sessions
- Learn about tea production
- Buy authentic Longjing tea
- Visit traditional tea villages

### Suggested Itinerary

**Morning**
- Early train to Hangzhou
- West Lake exploration
- Boat ride

**Afternoon**
- Lingyin Temple
- OR Longjing tea plantations
- Lunch at lakeside restaurant

**Evening**
- Return to Shanghai
- OR stay overnight for sunset at West Lake

### Tips

- Spring (March-May) best for tea picking
- Summer: lotus flowers on West Lake
- Autumn: gorgeous foliage
- Can be done as day trip but overnight recommended
- Very popular - book trains early
