## Cabin Categories

### Suite (Top Deck)

**Best For**: Luxury travelers, special occasions

**Features**:
- Largest cabins (30-50 sqm)
- Private balcony
- Separate living area
- Premium bedding and amenities
- Best views
- Priority for dining and excursions

**Price Range**: $$$$

**Pros**:
- Maximum comfort and space
- Best panoramic views
- VIP service
- Quieter location

**Cons**:
- Significantly more expensive
- May have more motion (higher deck)

### Deluxe Cabin with Balcony (Upper Decks)

**Best For**: Most travelers seeking comfort

**Features**:
- 20-25 sqm
- Private balcony
- Large windows
- Modern amenities
- Comfortable beds
- Mini-fridge

**Price Range**: $$$

**Pros**:
- Excellent value
- Fresh air on balcony
- Good views
- Still spacious enough

**Cons**:
- More expensive than standard

### Standard Cabin (Middle Decks)

**Best For**: Budget-conscious travelers

**Features**:
- 15-20 sqm
- Large windows (non-opening)
- Twin or double beds
- Basic amenities
- Private bathroom

**Price Range**: $$

**Pros**:
- More affordable
- Still comfortable
- Less motion than upper decks

**Cons**:
- No balcony
- Smaller space
- Windows don't open

### Interior/Lower Deck Cabins (Budget Ships)

**Best For**: Extreme budget travelers

**Features**:
- 10-15 sqm
- Small or no windows
- Basic furnishings
- Shared facilities possible

**Price Range**: $

**Pros**:
- Cheapest option
- Least motion

**Cons**:
- Claustrophobic for some
- No natural light in some cabins
- May not be worth the savings for multi-day cruise

## Deck Selection

### Upper Decks (5-6)
- **Best views**
- More expensive
- **More motion** (if sensitive to movement)
- Quieter (away from engine)
- **Premium cabins and suites**

### Middle Decks (3-4)
- **Balanced choice**
- Moderate pricing
- **Less motion**
- Most standard cabins here
- **Recommended for most travelers**

### Lower Decks (1-2)
- Budget options
- **Least motion**
- May have limited views
- **Closer to engine noise** on some ships
- **Not recommended unless on a budget**

## Cabin Features to Consider

### Bed Configuration
- **Twin beds**: Can usually be joined
- **Queen/King**: Better for couples
- Confirm bed type when booking

### Bathroom
- All cabins have private bathrooms in 4-5 star ships
- Shower (most common)
- Bathtub (luxury suites only)
- Check if hairdryer included

### Balcony
- **Worth the upgrade** for multi-day cruise
- Fresh air and photo opportunities
- Private space to enjoy scenery
- Can watch gorges passing by

### Location
- **Mid-ship cabins**: Less motion
- **Forward/aft cabins**: May have more vibration
- **Avoid cabins near elevator** for quieter sleep

## Recommendations by Traveler Type

### Couples/Honeymooners
**Recommend**: Deluxe cabin with balcony (upper deck)
- Private balcony for romance
- Good value for the experience
- Comfortable size

### Families
**Recommend**: Standard cabins (connecting if possible) or suite
- Suites offer space for family
- Two standard cabins may be more economical
- Ask about family configurations

### Solo Travelers
**Recommend**: Standard cabin (middle deck)
- Adequate space for one person
- Good value
- Check for single supplements

### Budget Travelers
**Recommend**: Standard cabin (lower deck) on 4-star ship
- Skip the budget ships - not worth the savings
- Standard cabin on good ship better than deluxe on budget ship

### Photographers
**Recommend**: Deluxe cabin with balcony (upper deck, mid-ship)
- Balcony for tripod setup
- Upper deck for better angles
- Mid-ship for stability

## Booking Tips

1. **Book Early**: Best cabins sell out first
2. **Ask About Upgrades**: Sometimes available at check-in
3. **Read Reviews**: Check cabin-specific reviews
4. **Check Deck Plans**: Avoid cabins near loud areas
5. **Consider Ship Age**: Newer ships have better cabins
6. **Balcony Worth It**: Spending 3-4 days onboard makes it worthwhile

## What's Included in Cabins

### Standard Amenities
- Private bathroom with shower
- Air conditioning/heating
- TV (limited channels)
- Mini-fridge (deluxe and above)
- Safe (most ships)
- Life jackets
- Toiletries (basic)

### Not Usually Included
- Wi-Fi (extra charge, often unreliable)
- Mini-bar items
- Premium toiletries
- Robes (luxury only)

## Ship Categories

### 5-Star International Standard
- **Victoria Cruises**: Highest Western standards
- **Century Legend/Paragon**: Modern luxury
- Best cabins, facilities, and service
- English-speaking staff

### 4-Star Quality
- **Yangtze Gold series**: Good mid-range
- **President series**: Popular choice
- Comfortable cabins, decent service

### 3-Star Budget
- Various operators
- Basic but functional
- Consider carefully for multi-day journey
