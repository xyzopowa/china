## Suzhou Day Trip
### Jardins et Sites de Suzhou

80-90 km from Shanghai, accessible by high-speed train in 25-45 minutes.

### Getting There

**High-Speed Train**
- Departs every 15-30 minutes
- Journey: 25-45 minutes
- Book tickets in advance via app or station
- Multiple Suzhou stations - check destination

### Classical Gardens (UNESCO)

**Humble Administrator's Garden**
- Largest classical Chinese garden
- Ponds, pavilions, and rock formations
- Ming Dynasty design
- 2-3 hours to explore

**Master of the Nets Garden**
- Smaller, refined design
- Evening performances sometimes available
- Example of Ming Dynasty landscaping

**Lion Grove Garden**
- Famous for elaborate rock formations
- Labyrinth of rockeries
- Less crowded than Humble Administrator

### Historic Streets

**Pingjiang Road**
- Canal-side historic street
- Traditional architecture
- Shops, tea houses, cafes
- Great for strolling

**Shantang Street & Grand Canal**
- Ancient canal quarter
- Boat rides available
- Evening lanterns
- Traditional shops

### Suggested Itinerary

**Morning**
- Take early train from Shanghai
- Visit Humble Administrator's Garden
- Walk Pingjiang Road

**Afternoon**
- Lunch at traditional restaurant
- Master of the Nets or Lion Grove Garden
- Shantang Street

**Evening**
- Return to Shanghai (trains run late)

### Tips

- Full day trip recommended
- Gardens require 2-3 hours each
- Wear comfortable shoes
- Combine gardens with historic streets
- Consider staying overnight to see evening illuminations
