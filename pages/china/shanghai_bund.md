## The Bund (Waitan)

Shanghai's most famous landmark - a waterfront promenade showcasing colonial-era architecture facing the futuristic Pudong skyline.

### What to See

- **Historic Buildings**: 52 buildings in various architectural styles (Gothic, Baroque, Romanesque, Art Deco)
- **Pudong Skyline**: Best views of the Oriental Pearl Tower, Shanghai Tower, and Jin Mao Tower
- **Huangpu River**: Watch boats passing on China's busiest urban river

### Best Times to Visit

- **Sunrise**: Peaceful, see locals exercising
- **Sunset**: Golden light on historic buildings
- **Night**: Buildings illuminated, Pudong light show

### Activities

- **Walk the Promenade**: 1.5 km stretch along the river
- **Photography**: Iconic Shanghai views
- **Historic Tours**: Learn about colonial history
- **Cross by Ferry**: Traditional ferry to Pudong (cheap and authentic)

### Practical Tips

- **Duration**: 1-2 hours for a leisurely walk
- **Metro**: East Nanjing Road (Lines 2, 10)
- **Free**: No entrance fee
- **Crowds**: Very busy evenings and weekends
