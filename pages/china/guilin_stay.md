## Where to Stay in the Guilin Area

### Guilin City

**Pros**:
- More hotel options
- Starting point for Li River cruises
- City amenities
- Transportation hub

**Cons**:
- Less scenic than Yangshuo
- More urban

**Best For**: Convenience, cruise departure

### Yangshuo

**Pros**:
- Stunning karst scenery
- Laid-back atmosphere
- Cycling and activities
- West Street nightlife

**Cons**:
- Smaller town
- Need to travel to/from Guilin

**Best For**: Scenic stays, outdoor activities

### Split Stay Option

- **Day 1-2**: Guilin (Reed Flute Cave, city sights)
- **Li River Cruise**: Guilin to Yangshuo
- **Day 3-4**: Yangshuo (cycling, river activities)
- **Return**: Bus or car back to Guilin

## Accommodation Tips

- Book river-view rooms in Yangshuo for karst views
- Yangshuo West Street can be noisy - stay slightly away if prefer quiet
- Guilin has more international hotel chains
- Yangshuo offers charming guesthouses
