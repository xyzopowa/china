## Advance Bookings Required

### Forbidden City
- **Booking Window**: Up to 7 days in advance
- **Daily Limit**: 80,000 tickets
- **Book at**: [Official Website](https://intl.dpm.org.cn/index.html?l=en)
- **Note**: Can sometimes purchase on-site if daily limit not reached

### Summer Palace
- **Booking Window**: 6 days in advance
- **Recommended**: Book via Trip.com or official site
- **Can book on-spot during off-peak periods**

### Temple of Heaven
- **Booking**: Advance booking recommended during busy periods
- **Options**: 
  - Entrance ticket only
  - Combined ticket (Hall of Prayer, Echo Wall Circle)
- **Book at**: Klook or Trip.com

## Recommended Pre-Booking

- **Great Wall (Mutianyu)**: Book tours or tickets 2-3 days in advance
- **798 Art District**: No booking needed, free entry to district (individual galleries may charge)
- **Parks**: Most can purchase tickets on-site

## General Tips

- Book major attractions as early as possible
- Have your passport information ready for bookings
- Download confirmation emails/tickets to your phone
- Some attractions require Chinese ID or passport for entry
