## Advance Bookings

### Major Attractions

**Shanghai Tower**  
- Book online for skip-the-line access
- Skywalk requires separate booking

**Shanghai Museum**
- Free but requires advance reservation
- Book several days ahead during peak season

**Yu Garden**
- Can purchase on-site but expect queues
- Morning visits recommended

### Day Trips

**Suzhou & Water Towns**
- Book high-speed train tickets in advance
- Train frequency: Every 15-30 minutes
- Journey time: 25-45 minutes to Suzhou

**Hangzhou**
- Book train tickets 1-2 weeks ahead for weekends
- Journey time: 1.5-2 hours

## Shows & Experiences

- **ERA Acrobatic Show**: Book 1-2 days in advance
- **River Cruises**: Book dinner cruises ahead for best seats
- **Cooking Classes**: Reserve at least 3 days in advance

## Transportation

- **Airport Maglev**: Can purchase on-site
- **Metro Card**: Buy upon arrival at any station
