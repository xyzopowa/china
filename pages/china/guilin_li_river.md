## Li River Cruise

The quintessential Guilin experience - a scenic cruise through dramatic karst peaks featured on the 20 RMB note.

### The Journey

- **Route**: Guilin to Yangshuo
- **Duration**: 4-5 hours
- **Distance**: 83 km (52 miles)
- **Scenery**: Limestone karst peaks, water buffalo, fishermen, villages

### What You'll See

- **Nine Horses Fresco Hill**: Rock formation resembling horses
- **Yellow Cloth Shoal**: Reflection point (20 RMB note scene)
- **Fishermen with Cormorants**: Traditional fishing method
- **Endless Karst Peaks**: The landscape that made Guilin famous

### Cruise Options

**Large Tourist Boats**
- Most common
- Multiple decks
- Meals included
- Narration (Chinese/English)

**Smaller Boats**
- More intimate
- Fewer passengers
- Higher cost

**Bamboo Rafting**
- Shorter sections
- Usually Yangshuo area
- Very peaceful
- Weather dependent

### Practical Information

**Departure**
- Morning departures common (8-9 AM)
- Pier: Zhujiang or Mopanshan

**Ending**
- Yangshuo town
- Return to Guilin by bus (1.5 hours)

**Weather**
- Best: Clear or slightly misty
- Rain can enhance atmosphere but limits visibility
- Check forecast

**Booking**
- **Advance**: 3-7 days recommended
- **Peak Season**: Earlier booking essential
- **Includes**: Usually lunch on boat

### Tips

- Sit on upper deck for best views
- Morning light better for photography
- Bring layers - can be cool on water
- Battery pack for camera/phone
- Some Chinese cruise commentary only
- The journey itself is the attraction - slow pace is intentional

### Alternative

**Yangshuo to Xingping Section**
- Shorter cruise or bamboo raft
- Most scenic section
- Less expensive
- Can be done as day trip from Yangshuo
