## Yu Garden (Yuyuan)
### Jardin du Mandarin Yu

A stunning example of classical Chinese garden design dating back to 1559.

### Garden Features

- **Rock Formations**: Intricately arranged Taihu stones
- **Pavilions**: Traditional Chinese architecture
- **Ponds**: Koi fish and water features
- **Dragon Wall**: Ornate decorative walls

### Highlights

- Exquisite Hall (Dianchun Tang)
- Grand Rockery
- Inner Garden
- Traditional corridors and bridges

### Surrounding Area

**Old City Bazaar**
- Traditional architecture
- Souvenir shops
- Street food vendors
- Tea houses

**Chenghuangmiao (City God Temple)**
- Taoist temple complex
- Traditional snacks

### Visiting Tips

- **Time**: 2-3 hours including bazaar
- **Best Time**: Early morning before crowds
- **Entry**: Admission fee required
- **Peak Times**: Very crowded weekends and holidays

### How to Get There

- **Metro**: Yuyuan Garden Station (Line 10)
- **Walking**: From the Bund (20-25 minutes)
