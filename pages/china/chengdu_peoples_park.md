## People's Park
### Le Parc du Peuple

Central park famous for traditional tea culture and local life.

### What to Experience

**Tea Houses**
- Traditional bamboo chairs
- Unlimited tea refills
- Ear cleaning services (traditional practice)
- People watching
- Mahjong games

**Local Activities**
- Tai chi practice (mornings)
- Dancing groups
- Calligraphy practice
- Kite flying
- Marriage market (weekends - parents seeking matches for children)

**Monument to Railway Protection Movement**
- Historical significance
- Beautiful park feature

### Tea House Experience

- Order tea (bamboo cup with lid)
- Relax for hours
- Watch locals
- Chat with staff (if speak Chinese)
- Affordable prices

### Visiting Tips

- **Best Time**: Morning for activities, afternoon for tea
- **Duration**: 1-2 hours minimum, or half day for full experience
- **Cost**: Very affordable
- **Location**: Central Chengdu, easy metro access

### What to Bring

- Book or entertainment
- Camera
- Patience to slow down and enjoy

### Nearby

- Tianfu Square
- Shopping streets
- Museum of Sichuan Province

### Why Visit

Authentic Chengdu lifestyle experience - see how locals spend leisure time.
