## Shanghai Art & Culture

### M50 Art District
### Art et Street Culture

Former factory complex transformed into galleries and artist studios featuring contemporary Chinese art.

**Highlights**:
- Contemporary art galleries
- Underground Chinese artists
- Giant street art murals
- Ephemeral exhibitions
- Raw, industrial atmosphere

**Location**: Moganshan Road  
**Entry**: District free, individual galleries may charge  
**Best For**: Contemporary art lovers

### Yuz Museum (West Bund)

Massive industrial hangars hosting large-scale installations.

**Features**:
- XXL art installations
- Suspended car sculptures
- Interactive pieces
- Post-industrial setting

### Tianzifang

Labyrinth of alleyways in the French Concession.

**By Night**:
- Hidden bars
- Fluorescent graffiti
- Street performances
- Bohemian atmosphere

**By Day**:
- Boutique shopping
- Cafes and restaurants
- Art galleries
