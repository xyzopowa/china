## Shanghai Maglev
### Prendre le Maglev - Train Suspendu

Experience the world's fastest commercially operated train, reaching speeds of 430 km/h (267 mph).

### The Experience

- **Top Speed**: 430 km/h (267 mph)
- **Journey Time**: 7-8 minutes
- **Route**: Pudong Airport to Longyang Road Station
- **Technology**: Magnetic levitation - train "floats" above track

### Practical Information

**Tickets**
- Purchase at airport or Longyang Road station
- Single journey or return tickets available
- Discount with flight ticket (same day)

**Operating Hours**
- First train: ~6:45 AM
- Last train: ~9:30 PM
- Frequency: Every 15-20 minutes

**Connections**
- **Longyang Road**: Metro Lines 2, 7, 16, 18
- **To City Center**: Transfer to Metro Line 2

### Tips

- Sit in first or last car for best views
- Watch the digital speedometer
- Quick way to/from airport
- Metro is cheaper but takes longer (45-60 min vs 30 min total with Maglev)

### Is It Worth It?

- **Yes If**: You value the experience and speed
- **Skip If**: Budget conscious (Metro is much cheaper)
- **Consider**: Taking it one direction for the experience
