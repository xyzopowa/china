## Nanjing Road
### Rue Commercante Nanjing Lu

Shanghai's most famous shopping street, stretching from the Bund to Jing'an Temple.

### East Nanjing Road (Pedestrian Street)

- **Length**: 1.2 km pedestrian zone
- **Character**: Historic shops, department stores, modern brands
- **Landmarks**: Peace Hotel, Sincere Department Store
- **Crowds**: Very busy, especially evenings and weekends

### West Nanjing Road

- **Character**: Upscale shopping, luxury brands
- **Modern**: Contemporary malls and boutiques
- **Less Crowded**: More sophisticated atmosphere

### Shopping Highlights

- Traditional Chinese products
- International brands
- Department stores
- Specialty shops
- Street food vendors

### Practical Tips

- **Best Time**: Evening for atmosphere and lights
- **Duration**: 2-3 hours for full walk
- **Metro**: East Nanjing Road (Lines 2, 10)
- **Watch**: Pickpockets in crowded areas
