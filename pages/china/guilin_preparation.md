## Advance Bookings

### Li River Cruise
- **Essential**: Book several days ahead
- **Peak Season**: Book 1-2 weeks in advance
- **Options**: Various boat sizes and classes
- **Tip**: Morning cruises offer best light for photos

### Reed Flute Cave
- Can usually purchase on-site
- Tours available with transportation

### Yangshuo Activities
- **Bamboo rafting**: Book locally in Yangshuo
- **Rock climbing**: Arrange through local guides
- **Cycling**: Rent bikes in Yangshuo town

## Day Trip Planning

- **Longji Rice Terraces**: 2 hours away, full day trip
- **Yangshuo**: Include in Li River cruise or separate day trip

## Tips

- Some attractions included in tour packages
- Independent travelers should book Li River cruise early
- Weather dependent - have backup plans
