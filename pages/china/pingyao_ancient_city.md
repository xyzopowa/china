## Pingyao Ancient City

A complete Ming-Qing Dynasty city preserved almost exactly as it was 300+ years ago.

### The City Walls
### Remparts et Vieille Ville

- **Length**: 6 km perimeter
- **Height**: 12 meters
- **Shape**: Tortoise (symbolic)
- **Towers**: 72 watchtowers
- **Walk**: Can walk or cycle entire perimeter
- **Views**: Panoramic views of old city
- **Time**: 2-3 hours to walk completely

### Main Street (Ming-Qing Street)

Historic commercial street through city center.

**Features**:
- Traditional shops
- Local crafts
- Tea houses
- Restaurants
- City Tower (central landmark)
- Beautiful at sunset

### Key Historic Sites

**Rishengchang Bank**
- First draft bank in China
- Pioneer of modern banking
- 19th century
- Fascinating financial history

- Taoist temple

**Qingxuguan**

### Evening Atmosphere

- Red lanterns illuminate streets
- Traditional music
- Street performances
- Less crowded
- Very photogenic

### Tourist Pass

- **Cost**: ~125 RMB
- **Includes**: Main 19 attractions
- **Valid**: 3 days
- **Buy**: At ticket offices near gates
- **Note**: Some smaller sites free without pass

### Practical Tips

- **Footwear**: Comfortable shoes (cobblestones, stairs)
- **Timing**: Morning or evening for best light and fewer crowds
- **Stay Inside**: Hotels within walls for full experience
- **Photography**: Everywhere - very photogenic
- **Crowds**: Weekdays quieter than weekends

### Getting Around

- **Walk**: Compact, entirely walkable
- **Bicycle**: Rentals available
- **Golf Carts**: For walls (expensive)

### Day Structure

**Morning**
- Walk city walls
- Visit main street

**Afternoon**
- Historic sites (banks, temples)
- Lunch at traditional restaurant

**Evening**
- Lantern-lit streets
- Dinner
- Photography
