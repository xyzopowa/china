## About Yichang (宜昌)

Yichang is a prefecture-level city in Hubei Province, strategically located at the entrance to the Three Gorges on the Yangtze River. It serves as an important hub for Yangtze River cruises and is home to the world's largest hydroelectric dam.

## Why Visit

- **Three Gorges Dam**: World's largest hydroelectric project
- **Yangtze Cruises**: Starting or ending point for river cruises
- **Natural Beauty**: Dramatic gorges and river scenery
- **Less Crowded**: Authentic local experience
