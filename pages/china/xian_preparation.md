## Advance Bookings

### Terracotta Army Museum
- **Booking**: Highly recommended, especially during peak season
- **Daily visitor limit**: Check current restrictions
- **Book online**: Official website or major travel platforms

### City Wall
- **Booking**: Can usually purchase on-site
- **Bike rental**: Available at multiple gates

### Dance Shows
- **Tang Dynasty Show**: Book 1-2 days in advance
- **Popular dinner-show combinations available**

## Tips
- Many sites require passport for entry
- Download tickets to your phone
- Arrive early for popular attractions
