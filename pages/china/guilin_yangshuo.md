## Yangshuo

The picturesque town at the end of the Li River cruise, surrounded by dramatic karst scenery.

### What to Do

**Cycling**
- Rent bikes and explore countryside
- Ride through rice paddies
- Visit local villages
- See karst peaks up close
- Full or half-day routes available

**West Street**
- Main tourist street
- Shops, cafes, bars
- International and local food
- Lively evening atmosphere
- Souvenir shopping

**Bamboo Rafting**
- Yulong River rafting
- Peaceful experience
- Close-up karst views
- 1-2 hour trips

**Rock Climbing**
- World-class karst climbing
- Guides and equipment rentals available
- All skill levels
- Moon Hill area popular

**Impression Sanjie Liu**
- Outdoor night show
- Directed by Zhang Yimou
- Karst mountains as backdrop
- Hundreds of performers

### Getting There

**From Guilin**
- Bus: 1.5 hours
- Li River cruise: 4-5 hours
- Private car/tour

**From Li River Cruise**
- Cruise ends in Yangshuo
- Stay or return to Guilin

### How Long to Stay

- **Day Trip**: See highlights, West Street
- **1 Night**: Add cycling, rafting
- **2-3 Nights**: Full experience, day trips to Longji, relaxation

### Nearby Attractions

**Moon Hill**
- Arch-shaped mountain
- Climb for views
- About 8 km from town

**Xingping**
- Old town
- Li River views (20 RMB note scene)
- Less touristy than Yangshuo town

**Longji Rice Terraces**
- 2 hours away
- Can be day trip or overnight
- Spectacular terraced hills

### Practical Tips

- Rent bikes from many shops on West Street
- Bargain for prices (cycling tours, rafts)
- Many travelers extend stay - it's that relaxing
- English more common here than other Guilin areas
- Great base for outdoor activities
