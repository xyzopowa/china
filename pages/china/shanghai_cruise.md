## Huangpu River Night Cruise
### Croisi√®re Nocturne

See Shanghai's spectacular skyline from the water, with the Bund and Pudong illuminated in a dazzling light show.

### Cruise Options

**Standard Cruise**
- Duration: 45-60 minutes
- Route: Along the Bund and Pudong
- Basic seating

**VIP Cruise**
- Premium seating near windows
- Sometimes includes drinks
- Better views

**Dinner Cruise**
- 2-3 hour experience
- Western or Chinese buffet
- Live entertainment or DJ sets on some boats

### What You'll See

- **The Bund**: Historic buildings lit up
- **Pudong Skyline**: Oriental Pearl Tower, Shanghai Tower, Jin Mao Tower
- **Bridges**: Illuminated river crossings
- **River Traffic**: Busy waterway

### Booking Tips

- **Advance Booking**: Recommended, especially for dinner cruises
- **Best Time**: 7-9 PM for light show
- **Weather**: Check forecast - better on clear nights
- **Seats**: Book window seats for best views

### Departure Points

- Various piers along the Bund
- Shiliupu Pier is popular
- Check your booking for exact location

### Alternatives

- **Ferry**: Much cheaper, no commentary but authentic
- **Sunset Cruise**: Earlier departure for golden hour
