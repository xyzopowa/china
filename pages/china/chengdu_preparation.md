## Advance Bookings

### Giant Panda Base
- **Booking**: Recommended during peak season
- **Best Time**: Morning (8-10 AM) when pandas are active
- **Duration**: 2-4 hours
- **Tip**: Book tickets online to avoid queues

### Leshan Giant Buddha
- **Distance**: ~2 hours from Chengdu
- **Booking**: Tour packages recommended
- **Duration**: Half to full day
- **Options**: Boat tour or walking path

### Jinsha Museum
- **Booking**: Can usually enter without booking
- **Closed**: Mondays

## Day Trip Planning

**Mount Emei** (1-2 hours away)
- Buddhist temple and monkeys
- Can be done as long day trip or overnight
- Cable car tickets available on-site

**Huanglong** (8 hours by bus)
- Colorful travertine pools
- Full day minimum
- Best April-November

**Mount Qingcheng** (train accessible)
- Birthplace of Taoism
- Half to full day trip
- High-speed train available
