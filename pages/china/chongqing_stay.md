## Best Areas to Stay

### Jiefangbei (Liberation Monument) Area
- **Pros**: City center, shopping, dining, metro access
- **Cons**: Busy, touristy
- **Best For**: First-time visitors, convenience

### Hongya Cave Area
- **Pros**: Iconic attraction, riverside, atmospheric
- **Cons**: Very touristy, crowded evenings
- **Best For**: Central location, river views

### Nanbin Road (South Bank)
- **Pros**: River views, less crowded, restaurants
- **Cons**: Across river from main center
- **Best For**: Quieter stay with views

### Shapingba
- **Pros**: University area, authentic local life, affordable
- **Cons**: Further from tourist sites
- **Best For**: Budget travelers, local experience

## Accommodation Tips

- Chongqing is very hilly - consider proximity to metro stations
- Hotels near metro lines are most convenient
- River-view rooms premium but scenic
- Book ahead during Chinese holidays

## Unique Stays

- **High-rise hotels**: Experience the vertical city
- **River cruises**: Stay on board for Yangtze experience
- **Hot pot hotel experiences**: Some hotels offer in-room hot pot
