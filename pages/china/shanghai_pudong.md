## Pudong District

### La Ville Futuriste

The modern face of Shanghai, featuring some of the world's tallest buildings and cutting-edge architecture.

### Iconic Skyscrapers

**Shanghai Tower (632m)**
- Tallest building in China
- Observation deck on 118th floor
- Includes Skywalk experience on 119th floor

**Shanghai World Financial Center**
- Distinctive "bottle opener" design
- Sky Walk on 100th floor
- Glass-bottomed observation deck

**Jin Mao Tower**
- 88-story pagoda-inspired design
- Observation deck and luxury hotel

**Oriental Pearl Tower**
- Distinctive sphere design
- Rotating restaurant
- Glass-floored observation deck

### Other Attractions

- **Shanghai Ocean Aquarium**: One of Asia's largest
- **Century Park**: Large urban park
- **Riverside Promenade**: Views back to the Bund

### Getting There

- **Metro**: Lujiazui Station (Line 2)
- **Ferry**: Cross from the Bund
- **Tunnel**: Bund Sightseeing Tunnel (touristy but fun)

### Tips

- Visit at different times - day and night offer different experiences
- Book observation deck tickets online to avoid queues
- Combine with a river cruise for best views
