## Best Areas to Stay

### Jinli Ancient Street Area
- Traditional atmosphere
- Close to Wuhou Temple
- Good restaurants
- Night market

### Kuanzhai Alley (Wide and Narrow Alleys)
- Historic hutongs
- Boutique hotels
- Cafes and bars
- Central location

### Chunxi Road
- Modern shopping district
- Metro access
- Modern hotels
- Nightlife

### Panda Base Area
- Quiet residential
- Close to pandas
- Further from city center
- Good for early panda visits

## Accommodation Tips

- Book near metro for easy access
- Traditional courtyard hotels offer unique experience
- International chains in business districts
- Hostels popular with backpackers
