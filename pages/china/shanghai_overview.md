## About Shanghai

Shanghai is China's largest city and global financial center, where East meets West in spectacular fashion. The city offers an intoxicating mix of historic architecture along the Bund, futuristic skyscrapers in Pudong, traditional gardens, and vibrant street life.

## Best Time to Visit

- **Spring (March-May)**: Pleasant weather, blooming flowers
- **Fall (September-November)**: Clear skies, comfortable temperatures  
- **Summer**: Hot and humid, but lively atmosphere
- **Winter**: Cool but manageable, fewer tourists

## Getting Around

- **Metro**: Extensive subway system covering all major areas
- **Maglev**: Experience the world's fastest commercial train from airport
- **Taxi/Didi**: Readily available, use apps for easier booking
- **Ferry**: Cross the Huangpu River between Puxi and Pudong

## Key Districts

### The Bund (Waitan)
Historic waterfront with colonial-era buildings and stunning Pudong views.

### Pudong
Futuristic district with iconic skyscrapers and the Shanghai Tower.

### French Concession
Tree-lined streets with colonial architecture, boutiques, and cafes.

### Old City
Traditional Shanghai with Yu Garden and historic neighborhoods.

## Must-Do Experiences

- Walk the Bund at sunset
- Take the Huangpu River cruise at night
- Explore traditional water towns nearby
- Visit world-class museums
- Experience the Maglev train
