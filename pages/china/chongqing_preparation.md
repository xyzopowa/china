## Advance Bookings

### Yangtze River Cruise
- **Booking**: Essential to book well in advance
- **Types**: 3-4 day cruises, luxury to budget options
- **Route**: Chongqing to Yichang (or vice versa)
- **Best Time**: Spring (April-May) or Fall (September-October)
- **Include**: Three Gorges Dam visit

### City Attractions
- Most can be visited without advance booking
- Hongya Cave can get very crowded evenings

### Transportation
- **Airport**: Chongqing Jiangbei International Airport
- **Metro**: Extensive network, get card upon arrival
- **Yangtze Cable Car**: Popular, may have queues

## Tips
- Book river cruises 1-3 months ahead
- Check cruise inclusions (meals, excursions, gratuities)
- Passport required for all bookings
