## Advance Booking Requirements

### National Forest Park
- **Booking Window**: Can book 7 days in advance
- **High Season**: Book as early as possible (April-May, September-November)
- **Peak Days**: Holidays and weekends sell out
- **Ticket Validity**: 4 consecutive days
- **Official Website**: http://www.zjjpark.com (Chinese)

### Tianmen Mountain
- **Must Book in Advance**: Limited daily tickets
- **Booking Window**: 7-30 days ahead recommended
- **Peak Season**: Book 2-4 weeks early
- **Time Slots**: Morning or afternoon sessions
- **Official Site**: http://www.tianmenshan.com.cn

### Glass Bridge
- **Advance Booking Required**: Strict daily limits
- **Book 1-2 weeks ahead**: Especially during peak season
- **Real-name Registration**: Passport required
- **Time Slots**: Specific entry times assigned

## Booking Methods

### Official Websites
- National Forest Park and Tianmen Mountain official sites
- Requires Chinese language ability
- Passport information needed
- Can be challenging for international travelers

### Third-Party Platforms
- **Ctrip/Trip.com**: English interface available
- **Klook**: International booking platform
- **GetYourGuide**: Tour packages
- Slightly higher prices but easier booking

### Through Hotel/Tour Operator
- Most convenient for international travelers
- Can arrange tickets as part of package
- May include guide and transportation
- Premium price but hassle-free

### On-Site Booking
- **Risky during peak season**: May sell out
- **Low season**: Usually available
- **Not recommended**: Especially for limited attractions

## Ticket Types and Prices

### Zhangjiajie National Forest Park

**Standard Ticket: ¥228**
- Valid for 4 consecutive days
- Unlimited park shuttle bus access
- Does NOT include cable cars or elevators

**Additional Transport Costs**:
- Bailong Elevator: ¥72 (one-way)
- Tianzi Mountain Cable Car: ¥72 (one-way)
- Yangjiajie Cable Car: ¥76 (one-way)
- Huangshi Village Cable Car: ¥65 (one-way)

**Total Budget**: ¥300-500 including park entrance and transport

### Tianmen Mountain

**Standard Ticket: ¥261**
- Includes cable car (up and down)
- Includes shuttle bus
- Choose route when booking (A, B, or C line)

**Route Options**:
- **Line A**: Cable car up, bus down (most popular)
- **Line B**: Bus up, cable car down (reverse)
- **Line C**: Cable car up and down (no bus on 99 bends road)

### Glass Bridge

**Standard Ticket: ¥138**
- Includes bridge entrance
- Sometimes bundled with canyon activities
- Check what's included when booking

### Discounts
- Seniors (60+): Usually 50% off
- Students: 50% off (with valid student ID)
- Children: Free or discounted (height-based)
- Disabilities: Various discounts

## Multi-Day Itinerary Planning

### 2-Day Itinerary
- **Day 1**: Zhangjiajie National Forest Park (Yuanjiajie + Tianzi Mountain)
- **Day 2**: National Forest Park (Golden Whip Stream) OR Tianmen Mountain

### 3-Day Itinerary (Recommended)
- **Day 1**: National Forest Park - Yuanjiajie & Avatar Mountain
- **Day 2**: National Forest Park - Tianzi Mountain & Yangjiajie
- **Day 3**: Tianmen Mountain

### 4-Day Itinerary (Comprehensive)
- **Day 1**: National Forest Park - Golden Whip Stream, Yuanjiajie
- **Day 2**: National Forest Park - Tianzi Mountain
- **Day 3**: Tianmen Mountain
- **Day 4**: Glass Bridge + relaxed exploration or Huanglong Cave

## What to Book in Advance

### Must Book Early (1-4 Weeks)
1. Tianmen Mountain tickets (time slot specific)
2. Glass Bridge tickets (limited daily capacity)
3. Mountain-top hotels (if staying overnight in park)

### Should Book Early (1 week)
1. National Forest Park entrance
2. Hotels in Wulingyuan
3. Return transportation

### Can Book On Arrival (Low Season Only)
1. Cable cars and elevators (pay on-site)
2. Additional activities

## Accommodation Booking

### Where to Stay

**Wulingyuan Town** (Recommended)
- Right at park entrance
- Many hotel options
- Best for multi-day park visits
- Book 1-2 weeks ahead

**Zhangjiajie City**
- Wider range of hotels
- 45 minutes to park
- Good if also visiting Tianmen Mountain
- More dining options

**On the Mountain**
- Limited guesthouses on Tianzi Mountain
- Basic facilities
- Amazing sunrise/sunset access
- **Must book well in advance**

## Essential Preparations

### Documents
- Passport (required for all bookings and entry)
- Booking confirmations (print or digital)
- Student ID (if applicable for discounts)

### Physical Preparation
- Moderate fitness required
- Practice climbing stairs
- Break in hiking shoes before trip

### Gear to Bring
- **Comfortable hiking shoes** (essential)
- Lightweight rain jacket (weather unpredictable)
- Layers (temperature varies with elevation)
- Sun protection (hat, sunscreen, sunglasses)
- Backpack for day trips
- Water bottle
- Snacks and energy bars
- Phone battery pack (GPS, photos)
- Small first-aid kit

### Photography Gear
- Camera with extra batteries (charging limited on mountain)
- Memory cards
- Lens cleaning cloth (misty conditions)
- Lightweight tripod (for sunrise/sunset)

## Weather Considerations

### Check Before Booking
- **Cloud cover**: Can obscure views completely
- **Rain**: Common in spring and summer
- **Autumn**: Typically clearest weather
- **Winter**: Snow possible, mystical but cold

### Flexible Planning
- Consider booking refundable options
- Have backup days if weather is poor
- Early morning often clearest

## Money and Payments

### Bring Cash
- Not all vendors accept cards
- ATMs in Wulingyuan and Zhangjiajie city
- Limited ATMs on mountain

### Budget Estimate
- Park tickets and transport: ¥300-500
- Food per day: ¥100-200
- Accommodation: ¥150-500/night
- Total per person: ¥600-1,200/day

## Food Planning

### In the Park
- Restaurants at main areas (expensive)
- Bring snacks and water
- Bottled water: ¥5-10 (¥3 in town)
- Simple meals: ¥30-60

### Strategy
- Big breakfast before entering park
- Pack lunch/snacks
- Early dinner after exiting park

## Language Preparation

- Download translation app
- Save hotel/attraction addresses in Chinese
- Learn basic phrases
- Park maps available in English

## Booking Timeline Example

### 4 Weeks Before
- Research and plan itinerary
- Book accommodation
- Purchase flights/train tickets

### 2-3 Weeks Before
- Book Tianmen Mountain tickets
- Book Glass Bridge tickets
- Reserve any tours

### 1 Week Before
- Book National Forest Park tickets
- Confirm all bookings
- Check weather forecast

### 1-2 Days Before
- Download offline maps
- Prepare gear and clothing
- Print/save all confirmations

## Important Notes

- Real-name registration required (passport)
- Tickets are non-transferable
- Arrive early to avoid queues
- Peak season = long wait times for elevators/cable cars
- Some attractions close in bad weather
- Insurance recommended for adventure activities
