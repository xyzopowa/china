## About Guilin

Guilin is renowned for its otherworldly karst mountain landscape, which has inspired Chinese artists and poets for centuries. The city serves as a base for exploring the Li River and surrounding natural wonders.

### Best Time to Visit

- **Spring (April-May)**: Comfortable weather, misty mornings
- **Summer**: Lush and green, occasional rain
- **Fall (September-October)**: Clear skies, best photography
- **Winter**: Fewer tourists, occasional fog creates mystical atmosphere

### Must-Do Experiences

- **Li River Cruise**: From Guilin to Yangshuo
- **Reed Flute Cave**: Illuminated stalactites and stalagmites
- **Karst Landscape**: Iconic limestone peaks
- **Yangshuo**: Laid-back town with cycling and climbing

### Getting There

- **Airport**: Guilin Liangjiang International Airport
- **High-Speed Train**: Connected to major Chinese cities
- **Within Guilin**: Buses, taxis, bike rentals

### Practical Tips

- English limited outside tourist areas
- Bring cash for smaller venues
- Rent bikes to explore countryside
- Book Li River cruise in advance
