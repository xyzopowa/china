## About Chongqing (重庆)

Chongqing is one of China's four direct-controlled municipalities and the largest city in southwest China. Built on mountains at the confluence of the Yangtze and Jialing rivers, this "Mountain City" is famous for its dramatic topography, spicy cuisine, and role as the starting point for Three Gorges cruises.

## Why Visit

- **Unique Geography**: Built on steep hillsides with incredible views
- **Hot Pot Capital**: Birthplace of Sichuan hot pot
- **Yangtze Cruises**: Gateway to Three Gorges cruises
- **Modern Skyline**: Futuristic architecture
- **Authentic Culture**: Less touristy than other major cities
