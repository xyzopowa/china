## Best Areas to Stay

### Near the City Wall
- Close to major attractions
- Traditional atmosphere
- Good restaurant options

### Muslim Quarter
- Vibrant food scene
- Cultural experience
- Central location

### Modern Districts
- Modern amenities
- Shopping centers
- Business facilities

## Accommodation Types

- **Boutique hotels**: In converted traditional buildings
- **Modern hotels**: International chains near business districts
- **Hostels**: Budget-friendly options in central areas
