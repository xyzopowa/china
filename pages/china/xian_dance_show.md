## Tang Dynasty Dance Show
### Spectacle de Danse

Experience the grandeur of Tang Dynasty culture through music, dance, and costumes.

### Show Details

- Traditional Tang Dynasty performances
- Elaborate costumes and choreography
- Often combined with dinner options
- Evening performances

### Booking

- Reserve 1-2 days in advance
- Multiple venues available
- Dinner-show packages recommended

### What to Expect

- 60-90 minute performance
- Traditional Chinese instruments
- Colorful costumes
- Historical storytelling through dance
