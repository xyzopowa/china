## Leshan Giant Buddha
### Grand Bouddha de Leshan

UNESCO World Heritage site featuring a 71-meter (233 ft) tall Buddha carved from a cliff face.

### About the Buddha

- **Height**: 71 meters (233 feet)
- **Built**: 713-803 AD (Tang Dynasty)
- **Location**: Confluence of three rivers
- **Purpose**: Protect boats navigating the rivers

### Viewing Options

**Walking Path**
- Descend stairs beside the Buddha
- See it from top to bottom
- Close-up views
- Can be crowded
- Takes 1-2 hours

**Boat Tour**
- View from the river
- Best for photos
- See full scale from distance
- Shorter time commitment
- Can combine with walking path

### Getting There from Chengdu

**By Tour**
- Most convenient option
- Includes transportation and guide
- Half-day or full-day options

**By Public Transport**
- High-speed train or bus (2 hours)
- Then local bus to scenic area
- More economical but time-consuming

### Nearby

**Mount Emei**
- Can combine in 2-day trip
- Buddhist sacred mountain
- Cable cars to summit

### Tips

- Morning arrival recommended
- Wear comfortable shoes (many stairs)
- Bring water
- Allow 3-4 hours minimum
- Can be very crowded on weekends/holidays
- Photography allowed
