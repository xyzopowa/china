## About This Travel Guide

This website is your comprehensive guide to planning a trip to China in 2026. Our goal is to provide you with modular, flexible itineraries that you can customize based on your interests, time, and budget.

### What Makes This Guide Different?

- **Modular Planning**: Mix and match activities and destinations
- **Flexible Options**: Choose from multiple alternatives for each day
- **Detailed Information**: Everything from transportation to dining recommendations
- **Up-to-Date**: Current information for 2026 travel

### How to Use This Guide

1. Browse destination pages for major cities
2. Explore sub-pages for specific activities and options
3. Build your own custom itinerary based on your preferences
4. Adjust as you go - all options are designed to be flexible

Happy travels!
