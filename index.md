## Welcome to China 2026

This website is your complete resource for planning a trip to China in 2026. Whether you're interested in ancient history, natural wonders, modern cities, or culinary adventures, our flexible itineraries help you customize your perfect journey.

## Featured Destinations

### Beijing
Explore China's capital city with visits to:
- **Dongcheng District**: Forbidden City, Tiananmen Square, Temple of Heaven
- **Gubei Water Town**: Picturesque water town near the Great Wall
- **Mutianyu Great Wall**: Less crowded section of the Great Wall with stunning views
- **Chaoyang District**: Modern Beijing, 798 Art District, shopping

### Xi'an
Discover ancient China in this historic city:
- **Terracotta Army**: Marvel at the 8,000 life-sized warriors
- **Mt Huashan**: Challenge yourself with one of China's sacred mountains
- **Xian City**: Ancient city walls, Muslim Quarter, Bell Tower

### Chongqing
Experience the mountain city:
- Hot pot capital of China
- Yangtze River cruises
- Urban exploration

### Yichang
Gateway to the Three Gorges:
- Three Gorges Dam
- Yangtze River cruises
- Natural scenery
